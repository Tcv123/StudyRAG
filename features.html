The RAG System
Three colours. Total clarity.

Every topic gets rated based on your diagnostic performance. The app tells you exactly where to spend your revision time.

🔴

Red — Needs work

Not confident yet. Shows up first in your revision queue and gets the most practice questions.

🟡

Amber — Getting there

You understand the basics but it's not solid. Topics can drop from Green back to Amber over time.

🟢

Green — Confident

You've got this covered. Keep reviewing to keep it green — it'll drop to amber if you ignore it too long.

How it works
From sign-up to exam ready

Six steps to take you from zero to confident — every topic in the green.

1

Create your account

Sign up and choose your level — GCSE, AS Level, or A Level.

2

Pick subjects & exam board

Select your subjects and your UK board — AQA, OCR, Edexcel, WJEC and more.

3

Take the diagnostic

10 questions per topic: 3 green, 3 amber, 4 red level.

4

Get your RAG rating

The algorithm places each topic automatically — no self-reporting needed.

5

Study & improve

Use notes, flashcards and practice questions to move topics up a colour.

6

Get everything green

Full green dashboard = fully confident and ready for your exam.
