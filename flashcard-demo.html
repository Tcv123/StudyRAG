Flashcard Interactivity — Option A vs Option B

Click the cards to flip. Try both — the difference is what happens after you answer.

Option A — Flip + Known/Unknown

Simple · Start here

Card flips. You mark it "Knew it" or "Didn't know." Unknown cards come back later in the same session. That's the whole algorithm.

Option B — Spaced Repetition (SM-2)

Advanced · Anki-style

You grade how well you knew it. The app schedules the card for a future date — tomorrow, 3 days, 2 weeks, 6 months — based on your grade and history. Cards you know well come back rarely; ones you struggle with come back soon.
